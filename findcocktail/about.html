About this website & Interaction on paper

This site is for introverted people and cocktail beginners! Imagine you're at a bar, instead of asking the bartender many questions, you can use this website to directly search for cocktails you may like. There are twenty labels on the website, which are the most common ingredients in cocktails. They cover a wide range of cocktails and won't make users feel difficult to choose.

The first interaction is a placemat. Instead of telling the bartender how you rate the drink, you can place your glass on the circles corresponding to the different ratings. Many people need such an implicit way of expression.

The second interaction is drawing lots. I designed a total of fifteen small cards, each with a creative idea. If you feel your cocktails are lacking a little creativity, pick a card randomly and follow the idea on it!
