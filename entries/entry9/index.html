WATER TOWER

The water tower is a tall structure used to store and distribute water. Water towers are common in cities, they successfully blend into the city yet stand out. Most water towers are built without considering their aesthetics since they are just practical buildings, but they have a special industrial aesthetic. The water tower is my favorite building. Unfortunately, these old water towers are disappearing. The original intention of their design was to meet the actual needs of water supply in the early days of the cities. With the development of the cities, they seem to be demolished as useless things after losing this value.
